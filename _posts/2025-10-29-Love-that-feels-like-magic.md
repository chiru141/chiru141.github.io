Love that they call Magic. Have you ever felt that? Have you ever felt that you were with someone and the feeling you felt for that person is pure magic? 
<!--more-->

I ponder upon this question as I watch - Sleepless in Seattle - A movie that has been on my watch list for about two years now. 
With Tom hanks and Meg Ryan as leads, who are famous for classics like sully, charlie Wilson\'s war, You\'ve got mail, When Harry met Sally and more. Who i admire and like. 

I watched the movie expecting the magic between them to unfold as they got to know each other. 
However, Annie feels the magic when he hears his voice on a radio (like hundred other women who fall in love with Sam because of his sensitivity). 

And Sam feels the magic when he sees her (for the first time at the airport). Even Sam\'s son Jonah feels this magic because of Annie\'s letter. 

I guess I was expecting too much from a 90s classic Tom Hanks\' movie. 
It\'s not like I haven\'t felt the magic at first glance in the last 28 years. I have, maybe more than once. But I do not think that any of us should be chasing that? Love is not that magic. 

Real magic which we called love for me is admiring the eyes of your person when they are describing that same incident the 100th time. Real magic happens when the laugh of your person makes you want to freeze time. You feel it when you miss the things they do to irritate you. 

So, call "Sleepless in Seattle" a classic all you want, I will still find "Set it up" richer in romance.
